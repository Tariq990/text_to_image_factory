The ancient library stood silent beneath a crimson moon. Dust motes danced in shafts of silver light that pierced the vaulted windows. Rows upon rows of leather-bound books stretched into darkness, their spines gleaming like sleeping serpents.

A hooded figure moved between the aisles, fingers trailing across the worn leather. They stopped at a pedestal where an open book glowed with faint blue luminescence. The runes on its pages shifted and writhed like living things.

Outside, thunder rolled across the mountains. Lightning illuminated the crumbling battlements of the old keep. Somewhere in the depths below, stone scraped against stone as something ancient awakened.

The figure closed the glowing book and tucked it beneath their cloak. Shadows lengthened as they hurried toward the eastern door, the weight of forbidden knowledge pressing against their chest. Behind them, the library settled into deeper darkness, holding its breath for what would come next.
